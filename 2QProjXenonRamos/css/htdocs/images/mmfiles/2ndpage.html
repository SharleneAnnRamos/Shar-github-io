The Covid-19 Pandemic


















Ever since the outbreak of coronavirus disease, it has greatly impacted our lives so much that it change the whole world. All of us have been affected by the pandemic, let us know the effects it caused upon us:

Health: The COVID-19 pandemic has become a global threat to public health, resulting deaths of millions. The pandemic has also affected the mental health of people. Many people during the pandemic have faced challenges and losses; such as isolation, loss of income, loss of love ones and fear. This may cause people to develop and suffer from different mental mental illnesses such as anxiety and depression. Causing harm to one's mental health

Economic: Not only the virus is a threat to public health, it has become a threat to the global economy. COVID-19 has killed millions of people. To prevent this, we have no choice but to go into lockdown and stay in our homes to prevent the virus spreading even more. Because of this, several businesses shut down and a rapid decrease of labour. The pandemic's disruption has caused millions of people to be at risk of poverty and unemployment.

Education: The pandemic has caused several schools to shut down. To continue learning for the youth, we have resulted to learn digitally. Although students are able to learn, there are also disadvantages to this. Students aren't to further develop their social skills, students would have a chance to cheat, students may suffer mentally due to the workload the school gives, and students have to do thing by themselves.
